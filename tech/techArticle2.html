What’s a Fitbit?

A fitbit is a smartwatch tshat tracks the persons step, heart rate, miles walked, and calories burned. The fitbit helps people to stay healthy or become more healthier and to stay motivated to move around.


Why Should You Get A Fitbit?


Keeps You Motivated:

When you buy the fitbit in the box it tells you to download their app to see your progress throughout the days and weeks. In the app it also has leaderboard where you can compete with your friends who have fitbits. According to Business Insider, “There's tons of stuff within the application, but one of the most interesting aspects is the ability to add friends who also have a Fitbit and compete with them for steps on a daily basis.” What attracts people that have fitbit to keep themselves motivated is to beat their friends on steps they take.

Tracks Your Exercise:

In the fitbit app it shows the person the exercises that they have done throughout the week. According to Business Insider, “Each of my actions came to be less about doing something I enjoyed — from enjoying the crunchy, sweet deliciousness of a mid-afternoon snack to sweating it out at a candlelit yoga class — and more about how it would weigh into a bigger, calculated view of my overall ‘fitness’.” It also let the user know the time they did Yoga and how much calories they burn. This would help people who want to lose weight and keep track of how much they have lost weight.

Reminders to Move Around:

In the fitbit for 8 or 9 hours in every hour it reminds you to walk so that you can reach 250 steps or more. According to fitbit blog, “The reminders nudge you to walk at least 250 steps each hour. You'll feel a vibration and see a reminder on your screen at 10 minutes before the hour if you haven't walked 250 steps.” This shows that it makes sure that the user keeps on moving to gain more steps throughout the day.

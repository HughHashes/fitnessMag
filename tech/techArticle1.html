Implementing VR Games Into Fitness In High School

  1. Home
  2. Technology

Virtual Reality is an artificial environment that is created with software and makes them believe that there in a certain place. An experience that only used two out of the five sense which is sight and sound. According to Fitness Insider, the benefits of VR in fitness is your able to set your temperature, no distractions, and avoid the negative parts of the gym crowds.

Stay Inside to workout:

Some teenager don’t like going outside when it’s summer or spring because it’s too hot and it makes them sweat a lot. A way to fix this is to have a VR fitness game. A game that have been used for fitness is knockout League. This will allow students to workout indoors and play online VR fitness games. While playing VR fitness game you are able to build your reflexes.

Weather Outside:

During the school year, some schools don’t have a indoor gym so they would workout outside when warm but when its winter they don’t workout. A solution for this problem is to buy equipment of VR like HTC or Oculus to play fitness game inside when it’s winter. So that students are able to workout indoors in the classroom.

Saves money on equipment for fitness:

Schools would save money on fitness equipment because the PC computers cost a lot when you first buy it but you would only have to pay for the price of the computer, games, and equipment. This also means that the schools doesn’t have to but a whole class equipment of fitness weights or other activities. According to Primo Fitness, “Although the cost of opening a gym varies greatly, you can estimate to spend between $10,000 for a personal training studio to $50,000 for a fully equipped commercial gym on equipment alone.” While a PC only cost $1,500 times the number of students in a class. This shows that buying PC are cost less than Fitness equipment.

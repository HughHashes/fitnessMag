What is Sweatcoin?

Sweatcoin is a cryptocurrency that you can earn when your walking or running. Every 1,000 step allows you to make 1 sweatcoin with these coins you can redeemed them for gift cards, healthy energy drinks, digital watches, running shoes, electronics, and more. This also, helps people to be motivated to keep on gaining steps.


Cryptocurrency:

According to Dummies, Cryptocurrency is virtual currencies but they aren’t like U.S. dollars, they live online and aren’t backed up by a government. In sweatcoin, your able to exchange the cryptocurrency with rewards that are available in the store sweatcoin which are mostly gift card, electronics, and discounts.

Motivation:

People are mostly likely going to walk or run if they made money for walking or running. According to CNN Money, “‘If you remember that your steps can be converted into something valuable, you're slightly more likely to walk rather than take a bus,’ Derlyatka said.” Also, the founders want to help the government and healthcare system to motivated people to be more active and so that they can save money.

Money/Gift Card:

When you noticed that you have been walking a lot and collected enough sweatcoins to buy something than you can exchange that currency for offers. Some of the offers given in sweatcoin is discounts for clothing, healthy food, exercise equipment, or even Amazon credit. Also, keep in mind that there isn’t always going to be good rewards in exchange for sweatcoin.

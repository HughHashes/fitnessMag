Top 3 Abs Burning Excercises by Kharis Spencer

  1. Home
  2. Blog Post

Posted on May 29, 2018 at 2:27 PM

These 3 Ab exercises work solely on your core and will give you that flat dreamy tummy 6 pack that you have been wanting. The ab exercises work on everyone. If you have always wanted your dream abs, this workout is for you. Below are my top 3 ab workouts to give you that dream stomach.

Plank Press - Targets your shoulders, back, triceps, and abs . Full push up position with arms shoulder-width apart, with hands directly under shoulders. Step feet out slightly wider than your shoulder-width. Extend right arm forward. Return right elbow to bent position and repeat. Do 10 reps, switch sides and repeat. Do 2 sets.

Monkey Arms - Targets shoulders, triceps, and biceps. Stand with feet hip width apart, holding a dumbbell in each hand, arms by side Raise dumbbells up along sides of rib cage to armpits, elbows pointing out to sides. Extend arms out to sides, palms down. Reverse position back to start Do 2 sets of 2 reps.

Diamond Push Up Works on triceps, chest, shoulders, and core. Walk your hands together so that your thumbs and forefingers form a triangle Bend your elbows to lower your torso toward the ground to do a complete push-up.

The Workout of Killmonger and Creed: Michael B. Jordan’s Workout

Michael B. Jordan has been in a lot of physically demanding roles in the past years. These roles have included Killmonger from the hugely successful Black Panther, Creed, and The Human Torch in the Fantastic Four (but we don’t talk about that last one). Michael was not always as strong as he is today, he has had to bulk up and gain a lot of muscle mass to be able to be in these roles. This is how he went from skinny twerp to buff as hell in just a few short months.


The first thing you need to know is that this workout isn’t easy. Michael does not just do a light workout every day. Michael B. Jordan spends a large part of 4-5 days of the week doing intense weight training, boxing, and other intense workouts to build up his muscle mass and strength. He spends the rest of the week going active be less intense activities like playing basketball casually. It is also worth noting that he has a very strict plan that he sticks to when he is working out.


Another thing you should know is that it is important to have a “recovery” period. After years of being in these extremely demanding roles, Michael has realized that it take a toll on your mental and physical health. For this reason, it is important to have one day of relaxation and peaceful activity every week. During this time it is beneficial to do things such as play a casual sport or meditate.


The final thing to be aware of is that this workout routine works best with a strong purpose and goal. When Michael B. Jordan does these intense routines he does it because he is preparing for roles in movies that make him millions of dollars.

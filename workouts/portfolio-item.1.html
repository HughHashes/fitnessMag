You need to lose weight fast? Take The HIIT

The HIIT workout is the secret weapon that Hollywood has up its sleeve. If you have ever seen a video of a celebrity moving swinging ropes up and down or doing 100 pushups right after doing 100 sit ups. Well then you have seen people doing HIIT workouts. HIIT workouts are exercise in which your body is doing an extreme amount of exercise in a very short period of time, usually leading to burning immense amounts of fat and large muscle growth.


The first thing that you need to know about the HIIT workout is that it is intense. This workout only requires about 20 minutes of intense workout every day. The caveat of this is that it will be the most intense 20 minutes of physical activity you will ever have. During those 20 minutes you do dozens or hundreds of reps with little to no to for breaking. However, if you are willing to put in the work for those few minutes the results will be phenomenal.


The immediate response to learning about this is that people think it is too good to be true, well it's true. The science behind this workout is that these 20 minutes of intense exercise start fat burn, and once it begins it stays active for the entire day. This acts as fat burning machine that can get you from Jack Black to the Rock in about 8 weeks. Examples of this workout are Battle Ropes, quick paised push up and sit up reps, and 200 fast paced burpees. Let's look at some of these in more detail (All examples can be found here).


In conclusion, HIIT workouts are the secret weapons of celebrities, that get them in the best and appealing states of physical fitness. HIIT workout, while short, very intense and it is important to remain hydrated while doing them. These workouts are not to be taken lightly, but if executed correctly these workouts build up muscle mass and strength to rival Chris Hemsworth.

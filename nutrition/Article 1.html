Diets

There are many different diets that high school students can do so that they are “healthy”. For example, some diets high school students can use are Low-Fat diet, Gluten-free diet, Juice fasting, etc. But I am going to tell you about the diets I believe that high school students will be able to do to lose weight, they are Vegan Diet/Fruits diet, and the water diet.

Vegan Diets/ Fruit Diet

A fruit diet is a highly restrictive vegan diet. In this diet it excludes animal products, including dairy. People who did this diet eat mostly raw fruits. You eat the following things: Vegetables Dried Fruits Nuts( chickpeas,almonds, peas, etc) Seeds Grains, legumes, and tubers are eliminated completely. Cooked food of any kind, including cooked fruit is avoided. Also the fruit diet carries many risks, such as malnourishment, so please consult your doctor about your intentions when doing this diet. They will be able to guide you while doing this diet.

Water Diet:

To do a water diet there is no restrictions on what you eat, the only stipulation is that water must be cold when you drink it. When doing this, the body burns extra calories heating up ice-cold water to the normal body temperature of 98.6 degrees, the colder the water the more calories burned. hese are two diets i believe high school students will be able to do.

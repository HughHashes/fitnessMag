Easy Recipes

There are many different types of easy recipes that children and adults can make who are trying out a vegan/fruit diet.There many other recipes you can do but I chose these two because in these there are many nutrients you get from these two meals Here I am going to tell you 2 different easy recipes that you can make that can help you keep going with diet that you are maybe doing.

Breakfast Recipe (PB&J SMOOTHIE)

Things You need:
  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • ¼ cup grape juice
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • ½ cup of unsweetened almond milk
How do You make it:
  • Add 1 scoop of protein powder
  • Add ¼ cup of grape juice
  • Add 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • Lastly add ½ cup of unsweetened almond milk to the blender

To make thicker, add ice and to make it sweeter, add some frozen berries or use sweetened almond milk. This meal gives you 21 calories from the Peanut Butter, 115 calories from the protein powder. This is important because it allows you to get calories but not alot that you are messing up your diet.

Dinner Recipe (Broiled Italian Salmon)

    Things you need:
  • Olive Oil
  • Italian Spices
  • Lemon Juice
    How do You make it: First you put the salmon into the olive oil that is in the pan
  • Then Add a bit of Italian Spices and lemon Juice to a 4-ounce fillet before broiling

Then if you want you can pair it with a baked potato wedges (skin on) for carbs and satiety and a veggie that you have in your fridge for fiber. These two recipes are two of many easy recipes that you could possibly make for you and your family if on a vegan diet. There many other recipes you can do but I chose these two because these are two recipes that I know that a high school student could make and could really understand.

Fasting

Many people believe that fasting can help you lose weight in a more healthier way other than going on a diet. But does fasting really help you lose weight and is it health for you to fast when trying to lose weight. Are there dangers when your fasting for weight loss? There are dangers when your fasting for weight loss because when you dramatically reduce your calorie intake, you will lose weight. But it can also cause many different health problems including muscle loss. Also when you start fasting, your body goes into conservation mode, buring calories more slowly . What if your doing intermittent fasting can you still lose weight? Doing intermittent fasting will lead to significant weight lose . But unless you compensate by eating much more during the eating periods, then you will be taking in fewer calories than you would normally if you were not fasting . According to a article i read it states that in a review, intermittent fasting was found to reduce body weight by 3-8% over a period of 3-34 weeks. Is fasting bad for your health? Fasting for a day or 2 is not really a problem if you are already on a health diet but it can be quite dangerous of you are not already eating healthy or on a healthy diet. And also if you have got liver or kidney problems, any kind of compromised immune system functioning or are on medication- even tylenol fasting is not really the best thing to do. Is fasting good for you metabolism? Fasting slows down your metabolism and causes your blood sugar levels to rise due to the stress fasting places on your body. Fasting incorrectly can do permanent damage to your metabolism, but metabolic fasting for fat loss is a healthy way to boost your metabolism, because it burns fat and helps shed pounds. In conclusion fasting has a lot of benefits but there are alot of health risks also. So, in my personal opinion i believe that intermittent fasting is better for those who want to do and are on a healthy diet.

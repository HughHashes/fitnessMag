Productivity Hacks Adara Mitchell

  1. Home
  2. Lifestyle

Productivity the state or quality of producing something is important especially when in school (college, high school etc). Being productive can easily give you peace of mind. Specifically if you have a office job or like stated before are in school you’ll be more stress free. In this article I will help you with school, life, and general hack to make your life simpler.

1. School Hacks

    Use Google Scholar to find paper and get alerts of study
    Learn how to study
    Use the pomodoro technique - You work in twenty-five minute increments, and after each take a five minute break. Stretch your legs, walk around the house a bit, do some sit-ups. Get your blood moving in some way, then go back to work.
    Stay organized
    Never be afraid to ask for help !!
2. Life Hacks
    Check your phone no more than once per hour
    Use a to-do list app
    Takele must-dos first
    Have a “no” strategy - Saying yes to every task leaves you overwhelmed, but saying no can be its own source of stress.
    Write down there of the most important tasks every morning !!
3. General Hacks
    Take a break you should shoot for working in short bursts at your most productive times.
    Set a timer for each of your tasks.
    Eliminate all distractions. This including the phone, email notifications, and having multiple web browsers open on the desktop.
    Just start. Most of the time, starting is the hardest part. Once you get going, you will quickly get into a rhythm that could last for hours.
    Get plenty of sleep !!

Overall following these simple hack can help you to achieve the perfect work/productivity ethic. You’ll be sure to have a peace of mind following these tips. Follow these tips and on your way to success!!

Mental Health by Dagmawit Zeleke

  1. Home
  2. Blog Post

Posted on August 24, 2013 at 9:00 PM

Mental health, a person’s condition with regard to their psychological, social, and emotional well-being. It is all about how we think, whether we have control over it or not. Anyone has the potential to develop a mental problem, no matter the circumstances. Almost 1 in 5 Americans develop a mental health issue each year. It is important that your mental health is in a good state. A large portion of people who have a mental disorder have more than one. A positive mental health allows people to have a better productivity rate, realise their full potential, make contributions to their community, etc.

Depression

Depression is a serious medical illness that negatively affects your mood. It is one of the most common disorder in the US. It can happen at any age, but usually during teen, or adult years. It can go away and reoccur depending on circumstances. Some symptoms of depression are irritability, anxiety or empty mood, feelings of guilt, worthlessness, helplessness, loss in interest in activities, etc.

How to Improve Mental Health

A mental issue often times makes people feel hopeless, so many people don’t try to seek help or think it’s worth it. The best thing to do is seek help whether it be professional or a trusted family or friend, stay positive, try to connect with others, help others, get enough sleep, and develop coping skills.

Harms of Mental health

Things like life experiences or biological features such as genes or brain chemistry cause bad mental health. A bad mental state can cause poor physical health, sedentary lifestyle, poor socio-eco status, poor diet, and high amounts of stress.
